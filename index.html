Introduction

Creative Bible Exposition is shaped for in-depth study and clear exposition. Each study begins with the Biblical text, follows the background, and turns it into a relatable sermon.

The aim is simple: Use Right and Left brain to make bible study exiting and effective without making it shallow, and make application concrete without losing the wonder of Scripture.
